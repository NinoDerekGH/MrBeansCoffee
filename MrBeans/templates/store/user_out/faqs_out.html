FAQS

Source and Quality:

Our coffee is serious on quality. We find only responsibly, sustainably, and ethically sourced coffee beans. We prefer family owned farms rather than corporate owned farms.

Specialty Grade Coffee:

We only order specialty grade coffee, which is the highest rating coffee can get. The coffee has to be almost free entirely from detects and standout in one attribute. To learn more about the Coffee Grading, click on this link to learn more: http://www.coffeeresearch.org/coffee/grade.htm

Roasting:

Mr. Beans Coffee is small batch roasted. The coffee you order have usually been roasted within 2-5 days of shipping to assure its freshness. For ideal taste, we recommend consuming the coffee within 2-3 weeks of receiving them.

Shipping:

We ship nationwide and have negotiated the best and fair rates we can with our carriers. We know some of the rate is higher than usual, the carriers set the rate at checkout based on what's in your cart and where it must be shipped.
